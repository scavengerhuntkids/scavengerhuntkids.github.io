Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application “Scavenger Hunt Kids – Pin Publisher” (the “App”) handles data. The App is a private, single-user tool operated by its owner to publish their own product pins to their own Pinterest business account via the Pinterest API.

1. Who we are

The App is operated by the owner of the Scavenger Hunt Kids product line (the “Operator”). Contact: gergely.andras1986@gmail.com.

2. What data the App accesses

The App connects only to the Operator’s own Pinterest business account using Pinterest OAuth. It uses this access solely to create boards and pins owned by the Operator. The App does not access, collect, or process data belonging to any other Pinterest user.

3. What data the App stores

The App stores a Pinterest OAuth access token locally, on the Operator’s own computer, only for the purpose of authenticating API requests. No user data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party. The App does not collect personal data from visitors or end users.

4. Third-party services

The App interacts with the Pinterest API. Use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

5. Data retention and deletion

The locally stored access token can be revoked at any time from the Operator’s Pinterest account settings, and the local copy can be deleted. No other personal data is retained.

6. Children’s privacy

The App does not collect any data from children. The products it promotes are printable activities intended for families; the App itself processes no end-user data.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to gergely.andras1986@gmail.com.
