Printable city scavenger hunts that turn a family trip into an adventure.

What we make

Printable PDF scavenger hunts for kids aged 7–12, designed to be played while walking a real European city. Each hunt guides your family through 7–8 real stops with fun facts, multiple-choice clues, and a secret word to collect along the way — no app required, works fully offline. Just print and explore.

What's inside each hunt

  • 7–8 real city stops with kid-friendly fun facts
  • Multiple-choice clues that build a secret word
  • A local legendary character to solve the mystery with
  • A parent answer sheet
  • Available in English, German, and Hungarian
